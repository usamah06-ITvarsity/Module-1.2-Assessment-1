INSECT HOUSE

Visiting the insect house at a zoo is like stepping into a miniature world teeming with life and diversity. As you enter, you're greeted by a symphony of buzzing sounds and vibrant colors. The exhibit is carefully designed to replicate natural habitats from around the globe, offering glimpses into the lives of insects rarely seen up close.
