ANIMALS MAP PLACES

FRAGILE FOREST

Nestled within the vibrant confines of the zoo, the picnic spot offers a serene retreat amidst the lively animal habitats. Shaded by ancient oak trees, picnic tables beckon visitors to unwind and indulge in homemade delicacies amidst the sounds of chirping birds and distant roars of majestic creatures. Families spread colorful blankets on the lush grass, while children's laughter mingles with the gentle rustling of leaves. From this vantage point, the view unfolds like a living tapestry: giraffes gracefully stretching for leaves, elephants splashing playfully in their watering hole, and monkeys swinging from branch to branch in joyful abandon

Where can the Picnic area be found?

Where ever there is a open fields with beautiful trees
